# Page Prompt: Talk to the AI - Contact Redefined

This page removes traditional forms and makes the AI voice assistant the primary contact path. It should feel like a direct line to an AI architect.

## Page Intent
- Convert serious intent into qualified conversations.
- Provide a calm, premium, voice-first experience.
- Offer a minimal fallback for edge cases.

## CTA Strategy (page level)
- Primary CTA: "Talk to the AI Architect" (voice).
- Secondary CTA: Minimal form.

---

## Section 1: Voice-First Hero
**Purpose**
- Establish voice as the default.

**Content Intent**
- Headline: "Talk to the AI Architect."
- Subtext: "No forms. Describe your system in your own words."

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Large central voice orb with subtle waveform motion.
- When hovered, the orb glows in the accent color.

**CTA Placement**
- Primary: "Start voice conversation."

---

## Section 2: How the AI Conversation Works
**Purpose**
- Reduce uncertainty and increase trust.

**Content Intent**
- Step 1: Understand the scenario.
- Step 2: Identify signals, data, and constraints.
- Step 3: Propose a system plan.
- Step 4: Schedule next steps if aligned.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Steps appear like a guided system dialogue.

---

## Section 3: Lead Qualification and Routing
**Purpose**
- Explain that the AI will qualify and route.

**Content Intent**
- "The AI only routes serious requests to a human."
- "You control what is shared."

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Use minimal system check indicators in amber.

**AI Voice Assistant Integration**
- Launch external voice app with context: "Contact -> Qualification".
- If qualified, transition to calendar or human handoff.

---

## Section 4: Fallback Contact (Minimal Form)
**Purpose**
- Support edge cases without compromising the experience.

**Content Intent**
- Name, email, one-line intent.
- Optional file upload for high-context cases.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Minimal form appears only after a "Need a form?" link.

---

## Section 5: Trust and Privacy Footer
**Purpose**
- Remove friction and reinforce safety.

**Content Intent**
- "Voice data is used only to build your system plan."
- Link to privacy and ethics details.
