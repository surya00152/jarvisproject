# Page Prompt: Procurement Pack

This page provides a clean, neutral access point to procurement documentation. It must feel like a trust resource, not a lead magnet.

## Page Intent
- Provide a clear list of procurement documents.
- Make access simple and respectful.
- Reduce legal and security review friction.

## CTA Strategy (page level)
- Primary CTA: "Request the procurement pack".
- Secondary CTA: "Ask the AI about a document".

---

## Section 1: Procurement Pack Hero
**Purpose**
- Set a calm, enterprise-ready tone.

**Content Intent**
- Headline: "Procurement pack, ready."
- Subtext: "Security, compliance alignment, and data handling details."

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Minimal, clean layout with a single action panel.

---

## Section 2: Pack Contents (Primary Section)
**Purpose**
- Show exactly what is included.

**Content Intent**
- Included documents:
  - Security overview
  - Compliance alignment summary
  - Data handling and retention policy
  - Reference architecture blueprint
  - Monitoring and support overview
  - Legal and accessibility summary

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Checklist format with quiet system indicators.

---

## Section 3: Access Method
**Purpose**
- Explain how to receive the pack without friction.

**Content Intent**
- Option A: Direct download (if allowed).
- Option B: Voice request for secure delivery.
- Optional: Minimal email form for audit trails.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Three small panels with clear labels.

---

## Section 4: Terms and Use
**Purpose**
- Clarify the use of the documentation.

**Content Intent**
- "For internal evaluation and procurement review."
- "No marketing follow-up unless requested."

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Small mono text block with a neutral tone.

---

## Section 5: Conversion Panel
**Purpose**
- Provide immediate access and assistance.

**Content Intent**
- "If you need a specific document, ask the AI."

**CTA Placement**
- Primary: "Request the procurement pack".
- Secondary: "Ask the AI about a document" (voice).

**AI Voice Assistant Integration**
- Launch external voice app with context: "Procurement pack -> Document request".
