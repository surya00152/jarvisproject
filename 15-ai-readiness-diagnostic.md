# Page Prompt: AI Readiness Diagnostic

This page is a guided readiness assessment that feels like an intelligent diagnostic interface. It should reduce sales friction and qualify leads without a traditional form.

## Page Intent
- Help visitors assess if they are ready for AI systems.
- Capture context through voice or minimal inputs.
- Produce a clear, honest readiness snapshot with next steps.

## CTA Strategy (page level)
- Primary CTA: "Run the AI Readiness Diagnostic" (voice-first).
- Secondary CTA: "Talk to the AI Architect".

---

## Section 1: Diagnostic Hero
**Purpose**
- Frame the assessment as a system check, not a quiz.

**Content Intent**
- Headline: "Run an intelligence readiness check."
- Subtext: "This takes 2-4 minutes and yields a system path."

**Design and Animation Logic**
- A minimal system scan animation with calm pulses.

**CTA Placement**
- Primary button: "Start voice diagnostic".

---

## Section 2: Signal Capture (Core Inputs)
**Purpose**
- Gather the minimum signals needed for a useful assessment.

**Content Intent**
- Questions about data availability, volume, and clarity.
- Operational pain points and decision latency.
- Integration constraints and privacy requirements.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Questions appear one at a time like a dialogue.
- Use mono text for system prompts.

**AI Voice Assistant Integration**
- Launch external voice app with context: "Readiness -> Signal capture".

---

## Section 3: Readiness Snapshot (Outputs)
**Purpose**
- Provide a clear, honest summary without overpromising.

**Content Intent**
- Readiness band: "Exploration", "Pilot Ready", "Production Ready".
- Key blockers and missing signals.
- Recommended next step.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Output appears as a system report card.
- Use amber highlights for blockers.

---

## Section 4: Suggested System Path
**Purpose**
- Move the user into an actionable path.

**Content Intent**
- "Recommended starting capability".
- "Proposed pilot scope".
- "Data connections required".

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Visual stack of recommended components.

---

## Section 5: Conversion Panel
**Purpose**
- Convert readiness insight into a real conversation.

**Content Intent**
- "If the diagnostic aligns, speak to the AI Architect."

**CTA Placement**
- Primary: "Talk to the AI Architect" (voice).
- Secondary: "Explore integrations".

**Note**
- Include a disclaimer: "This diagnostic is indicative, not a guarantee."
