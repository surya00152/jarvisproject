# Page Prompt: Live AI Experiences - Demo Hub

This page is a controlled gateway to live AI demo applications. It should feel like a lab or control room: calm, premium, and focused.

## Page Intent
- Convert curiosity into belief through real AI experiences.
- Make demos feel exclusive and intentional.
- Avoid distractions and marketing fluff.

## CTA Strategy (page level)
- Primary CTA: "Launch Experience" (per demo).
- Secondary CTA: "Talk to the AI Architect".

---

## Section 1: Demo Hub Hero - "Live Intelligence Lab"
**Purpose**
- Set the expectation for hands-on exploration.

**Content Intent**
- Headline: "Live intelligence. No simulation."
- Subtext: "Each module is a real AI system you can interact with."

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Background resembles a control-room grid with subtle system pulses.

---

## Section 2: Demo Modules Grid (Primary Section)
**Purpose**
- Provide clear, premium access to each external demo.

**Content Intent (per demo tile)**
- What it does (plain language).
- The AI capability behind it (technical hint).
- Expected outcome (what the user can experience).

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Tiles behave like lab modules, with status LEDs and system readouts.
- On hover, a short "system summary" appears in mono text.

**CTA Placement**
- Primary button: "Launch Experience" (opens external demo app).
- Secondary: "See how it is built" (links to process page).

---

## Section 3: Guided Exploration (Optional)
**Purpose**
- Help non-technical users choose a demo without friction.

**Content Intent**
- Short prompt: "Tell the AI what you want to see."
- The voice assistant can recommend a demo.

**AI Voice Assistant Integration**
- Launch external voice app with context: "Demo Hub -> Recommendation".

---

## Section 4: Demo Integrity and Trust
**Purpose**
- Assure visitors these demos are real and safe to explore.

**Content Intent**
- Small text: "No sign-up required. No data retained."
- Explain demo boundaries and privacy constraints.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Use minimal amber highlights to imply verification checks.

---

## Section 5: Conversion Panel
**Purpose**
- Provide a step to proceed after a demo.

**Content Intent**
- "If this is relevant, speak to the AI Architect."

**CTA Placement**
- Primary: "Talk to the AI Architect" (voice).
