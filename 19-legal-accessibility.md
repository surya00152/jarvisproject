# Page Prompt: Legal and Accessibility

This page provides essential legal and accessibility statements in a minimal, professional tone. It should feel like a clear system policy, not a wall of text.

## Page Intent
- Provide transparent terms and usage expectations.
- State accessibility commitments and contact paths.
- Keep legal language concise and human-readable.

## CTA Strategy (page level)
- Primary CTA: "Ask the AI about these policies" (voice).
- Secondary CTA: "Talk to the AI Architect".

---

## Section 1: Policy Hero
**Purpose**
- Set a calm, formal tone.

**Content Intent**
- Headline: "Clear terms. Accessible by design."
- Subtext: "Readable policies with direct support when needed."

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Minimal interface with a quiet system header.

---

## Section 2: Terms of Use (Summary)
**Purpose**
- Provide a short, readable summary.

**Content Intent**
- Acceptable use and prohibited activities.
- User responsibilities for data provided.
- Limits on liability and warranty overview.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Collapsible sections with concise summaries.

---

## Section 3: Privacy Reference
**Purpose**
- Link and summarize privacy practices.

**Content Intent**
- Point to the Privacy and Ethics page.
- Highlight data minimization and retention control.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- A short, linked summary block.

---

## Section 4: Accessibility Statement
**Purpose**
- State commitment to accessible experience.

**Content Intent**
- "Designed to align with WCAG 2.1 AA guidance."
- Provide a contact path for accessibility requests.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Accessibility iconography with high contrast text.

---

## Section 5: Policy Updates
**Purpose**
- Explain how policy changes are handled.

**Content Intent**
- Notify users of material updates.
- Provide a clear last-updated date area.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- A small system log entry with timestamp formatting.

---

## Section 6: Conversion Panel
**Purpose**
- Keep a human help path visible.

**Content Intent**
- "Ask the AI to explain any policy in plain language."

**CTA Placement**
- Primary: "Ask the AI about these policies" (voice).
- Secondary: "Talk to the AI Architect".

**AI Voice Assistant Integration**
- Launch external voice app with context: "Legal -> Explanation".
