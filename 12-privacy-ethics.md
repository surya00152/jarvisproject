# Page Prompt: Privacy and Ethics

This page establishes trust through explicit privacy, ethics, and responsible AI practices. It should read like a system policy console, not marketing.

## Page Intent
- Explain how data is handled, protected, and minimized.
- Show ethical boundaries and human oversight.
- Reduce risk concerns without over-claiming certifications.

## CTA Strategy (page level)
- Primary CTA: "Talk to the AI Architect" (voice).
- Secondary CTA: "Security and Compliance".

---

## Section 1: Trust Hero - "Ethics by Design"
**Purpose**
- Frame privacy and ethics as system-level design choices.

**Content Intent**
- Headline: "Ethics are engineered, not promised."
- Subtext: "Privacy, accountability, and control are built into every system."

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Minimal, calm interface with subtle system status indicators.

---

## Section 2: Data Minimization and Control
**Purpose**
- Show how data usage is minimized and controlled.

**Content Intent**
- Principles: collect only what is needed, keep only as long as needed.
- Explain user control over retention and access.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Visualize data flow with gates that close when not required.

---

## Section 3: Responsible AI Practices
**Purpose**
- Explain the guardrails that prevent harmful or biased behavior.

**Content Intent**
- Bias checks and evaluation.
- Human override and approval flows.
- Explainability and audit trails.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Guardrail lines appear as system constraints.

---

## Section 4: Transparency and Accountability
**Purpose**
- Clarify how decisions can be traced and reviewed.

**Content Intent**
- Model outputs are logged for audit.
- Decision pathways can be traced to data and logic.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Use a timeline of "decision events" with trace lines.

---

## Section 5: Voice Assistant Privacy Note
**Purpose**
- Reduce friction for voice-first interactions.

**Content Intent**
- "Voice data is used only to build your system plan."
- "No voice data is shared without explicit consent."

**AI Voice Assistant Integration**
- Launch external voice app with context: "Privacy -> Assurance".

---

## Section 6: Conversion Panel
**Purpose**
- Offer a direct path for deeper questions.

**Content Intent**
- "Ask the AI how we protect your data."

**CTA Placement**
- Primary: "Talk to the AI Architect" (voice).
- Secondary: "Review Security and Compliance".
