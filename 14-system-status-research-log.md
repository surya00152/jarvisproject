# Page Prompt: System Status and Research Log

This page feels like a live AI control panel and research log. It replaces a traditional blog with a transparent system feed.

## Page Intent
- Show ongoing AI system evolution without marketing fluff.
- Provide transparency into model updates and research direction.
- Strengthen trust by showing deliberate progress.

## CTA Strategy (page level)
- Primary CTA: "Ask the AI about the latest update" (voice).
- Secondary CTA: "Talk to the AI Architect".

---

## Section 1: System Status Hero
**Purpose**
- Set the "control console" tone.

**Content Intent**
- Headline: "System status: Active."
- Subtext: "A transparent log of intelligence evolution."

**Design and Animation Logic**
- A system status bar with subtle pulse indicators.
- Only one accent highlight per state change.

---

## Section 2: Live Status Indicators
**Purpose**
- Show readiness without promising uptime metrics.

**Content Intent**
- Indicators like "Model evaluation pipeline: active".
- "Inference monitoring: enabled".
- "Privacy safeguards: enforced".

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Minimal LED-style indicators; calm, not flashy.

---

## Section 3: Research and Update Log (Primary Section)
**Purpose**
- Provide a timeline of improvements and experiments.

**Content Intent**
- Short entries with date, focus, and outcome.
- Examples: "Improved RAG retrieval quality", "New voice latency optimization".

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Vertical timeline with system tick marks.
- Each entry expands to show technical details in mono text.

---

## Section 4: Intelligence Roadmap (Optional)
**Purpose**
- Show direction without making hard promises.

**Content Intent**
- Short list of "next focus areas".
- Avoid exact dates; use "in progress" or "up next".

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Roadmap items appear as queued tasks in a system queue.

---

## Section 5: Ask the System
**Purpose**
- Encourage interaction with the voice assistant.

**Content Intent**
- "Ask the AI to explain the latest update."
- "Ask about the current system capabilities."

**AI Voice Assistant Integration**
- Launch external voice app with context: "System Status -> Inquiry".

---

## Section 6: Conversion Panel
**Purpose**
- Provide a direct human conversation path.

**Content Intent**
- "If you want this system built for your organization, talk to the AI Architect."

**CTA Placement**
- Primary: "Talk to the AI Architect" (voice).
