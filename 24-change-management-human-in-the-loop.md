# Page Prompt: Change Management and Human-in-the-Loop

This page explains how humans stay in control and how teams adopt AI systems. It must feel operational and grounded.

## Page Intent
- Show how AI integrates with real teams and workflows.
- Explain human oversight, approvals, and role shifts.
- Build trust for operational and risk-aware buyers.

## CTA Strategy (page level)
- Primary CTA: "Ask the AI about adoption planning" (voice).
- Secondary CTA: "Review monitoring and support".

---

## Section 1: Change Management Hero
**Purpose**
- Frame adoption as a controlled transition.

**Content Intent**
- Headline: "Intelligence with humans in control."
- Subtext: "Adoption is engineered, not rushed."

**Design and Animation Logic**
- A staged rollout bar with clear gates.

---

## Section 2: Human-in-the-Loop Controls
**Purpose**
- Show where humans approve, review, and override.

**Content Intent**
- Approval gates for high-risk decisions.
- Escalation paths and manual review.
- Clear audit trails for every action.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- A decision tree with visible human checkpoints.

---

## Section 3: Role Transitions
**Purpose**
- Clarify how teams adapt to AI assistance.

**Content Intent**
- "From manual execution to exception handling."
- "From reactive support to proactive oversight."

**Design and Animation Logic**
- Two-column before/after role mapping.

---

## Section 4: Enablement and Training
**Purpose**
- Reduce adoption friction.

**Content Intent**
- Short training cycles aligned to roles.
- Clear feedback loops to improve the system.

**Design and Animation Logic**
- A simple loop: Train -> Use -> Feedback -> Improve.

## Section 5: Adoption Milestones
**Purpose**
- Show how adoption progresses safely.

**Content Intent**
- Pilot -> Controlled rollout -> Full production.

**Design and Animation Logic**
